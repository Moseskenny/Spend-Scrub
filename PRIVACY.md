# Privacy Policy for SpendScrub

**Last Updated: April 20, 2026**

SpendScrub is built with a "local-first" philosophy. We believe your financial data belongs to you, and you alone. This Privacy Policy describes how SpendScrub handles your information.

## 1. Data Collection and Transmission
SpendScrub **does not collect, store, or transmit** any of your personal or financial data to external servers. 
* All transaction details, account balances, and category information are processed and stored locally on your device.
* We do not have access to your financial records, and we do not sell or share any user data with third parties.

## 2. Local Storage
All data entered into the application (including data parsed via the MagicBar) is stored using a local database engine (IndexedDB) on your hardware. This data remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to export it or delete the application.

## 3. Third-Party Services
* **Analytics:** SpendScrub does not use any third-party analytics or tracking software. We do not monitor your usage patterns.
* **Cloud Sync:** By design, SpendScrub does not use cloud-based synchronization. Your data does not leave your device unless you perform a manual local export.

## 4. User Control
Since all data is stored locally, you have total control over your information:
* **Deletion:** You can delete your transaction history or the entire database at any time within the app settings.
* **Export:** You can export your data to a local JSON file for your own backups.

## 5. Permissions
SpendScrub may request specific device permissions (such as File System access for data export/import). These permissions are used strictly for the functionality of the app and are never used to harvest or transmit data.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
