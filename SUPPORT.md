# Support SpendScrub 🛡️

SpendScrub is built on the principle of **Local-First** sovereignty. It is a high-performance finance engine designed to keep your data exactly where it belongs: on your device. 

By sponsoring this project, you are supporting the development of private, open, and lightweight financial tools that don't rely on cloud tracking or data harvesting.

---

### 🚀 Why Sponsor?
Developing local-first software like SpendScrub—with its custom NLP MagicBar, Dexie.js persistence, and Rust-based core—requires significant time and research. Your support helps cover:
* Continued development of the **Pro Roadmap** (PeerSplit QR sync, VisionScrub OCR).
* Infrastructure for high-speed builds and CI/CD.
* Keeping the core application free and ad-free for everyone.

---

### ❤️ Other Ways to Help
If you can't support financially, you can still help SpendScrub grow:
* **Star the Repo:** It helps with visibility.
* **Share:** Tell others about a privacy-first way to track their capital.
* **Feedback:** Report bugs or suggest NLP improvements for the MagicBar.
